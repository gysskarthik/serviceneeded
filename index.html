My Summary

• 21 plus years of Academic experience in engineering education. Administrative experience as Dean and Head of the department for the past 10 years helped in creating a healthy and congenial academic environment in the organizations served till now. • Developed and implemented the techniques to extend the life-time of the wireless networks based on sensor node parameters and electrochemical characteristics of the battery. A National patent was registered for the same. • Second National patent was registered to improve the performance of the battery driven devices. • Third International patent was registered to improve the Battery Management System of Electric Vehicles.

Honored to be invited as one of the speakers at the following international summits: • 2nd World Conference on Robotics & Artificial Intelligence”, held during June 10-12, 2019 at Osaka, Japan. • 5th International congress on Energy efficiency and Energy related Materials” held during October 22-28, 2019 in Blue Lagoon, Fethiye/Mugla-Turkey. • 2nd World Summit on Advances in Science, Engineering and Technology jointly with Indiana University - Purdue University (IUPUI), Indianapolis, Indiana, USA held during October 3-5, 2019

• Reviewer of “Advances in Science, Technology, and Engineering Systems Journal”, (Scopus indexed) and “London Journals Press”. • Received best teacher award in the department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering of BIT, Ballarpur, India for the session 2014-15. • Expert teacher in the domains of Optical fiber communication, Satellite communication, Analog & Digital communication, and Signal processing & Image processing courses. • Delivered guest lectures in the domains of Signal and Image processing to benefit the students in rural colleges. • Encouraged the students to do the mini-projects related to the course work as an add on course. This strategy helped the students to understand the concepts practical orientation.

• Established “Digital Image Processing laboratory” and procured licensed software of ERDAS in 2007 at KITS, Ramtek, Nagpur, India. • Established the laboratory for “Electronic system design” in collaboration with STEP of IIT, Kharagpur, in 1999 at KITS, Ramtek, India. • Established “Digital signal Processing laboratory” using MATLAB in 1999 and subsequently upgraded with CCS at KITS, Ramtek, India. • Guided projects to the UG and PG students in the domains of Image processing, Secure Communication technologies. • Involved in organizing Inter-departmental competitions, Inter- collegiate competitions, Industrial visits, Guest lectures from Industrial experts, Workshops and Seminars in latest domains. • Adopted interactive based strategies and enquiry based learning methods in the class room, making the teaching and learning sessions lively.

• Successfully completed the Doctoral thesis work on “Investigations on development of wireless sensor networks for the extended lifetime performance”. • A part of the thesis work has been published as a book chapter by Springer-Verlag GmbH Germany, part of Springer Nature 2019 with the title “Energy efficiency”. • Published research papers in the domains of Wireless sensor networks, Image Processing, and Secure Communication, and Energy efficiency are published in Journals and International conferences. • Practitioner in Digital image processing and processed the images sent by Indian remote sensing satellite, in collaboration with the Regional Remote Sensing Centre, Nagpur.

To cater the needs of wider and heterogeneous entry behavior students, the teaching is tuned on the principles of Must, Should, and Could. • Following modalities are adopted while teaching the new concepts • Start from known and proceed to unknown • Start from general and move on to particular • Start from observation and move on to theory • Start with simple before making it complex • Start with an overview before introducing details With the above modalities very good results have been achieved where by students in a rural Engineering college of BIT, Ballarpur, Maharashtra, India, have secured University ranks competing with neighboring Government Engineering College. • To ensure a dynamic and stimulating, and effective teacher lead discussion, the questions are preplanned and are categorized as Learning and Control • Further, the questions are framed for higher class students based on Analysis, Synthesis and Evaluation. With these strategies, an unparalleled zeal is created, making the students more learning oriented.

• Designed and demonstrated the secure-communication without using the principle of synchronization for the first time at IIT, Kanpur, India, as a part of Masters Degree in Technology in 2005. • Designed “Microprocessor based Remote control panel for Precision approach Radar” for Hindustan Aeronautical Limited (HAL), Avionics Division, Hyderabad, India in 1992. This has reduced the cost of the then existing system to a significant extent.
